Tips for viewing comments

When viewing comments, try these suggestions:

To browse through your comments using the Comments List, click the Comments tab on the left side of the document pane. The options at the top of the Comments List let you expand, collapse, and delete comments, and more.

To view text associated with a comment, move the pointer over the markup.

To delete or reply to individual comments, open the pop-up menu and choose an option from the Options menu.
