Change the appearance of comments

To change the appearance of individual comments, such as their color, right-click (Windows) or Control-click (Mac OS) the comment, and choose Properties from the context menu. Specify the appearance options that you want.

To change the default appearance for a type of comment, such as notes or highlights, right-click (Windows) or Control-click (Mac OS) the comment, and choose Make Current Properties Default from the context menu. All subsequent comments that you create of that type will have the new properties.
